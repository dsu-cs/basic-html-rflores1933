Overview

Wolves are large carnivores that live and hunt in packs. They are known for their howling, teamwork, and strong social bonds.

Quick Facts

  • Wolves can run up to 40 miles per hour
  • A wolf pack is usually led by an alpha pair
  • They communicate through howls, growls, and body language
  • Wolves are found in North America, Europe, and Asia
